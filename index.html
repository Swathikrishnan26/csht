Nehru Arts And science College Kanhangad

Nehru Arts And Science College,Kanjhangad was established in the year 1966, Nehru Arts Science college, kanjhangad is affiliated to Kannur University, Kannur. The enlightened public of Kanjhangad and Nileshwar resolved to honour the memory of Jawaharlal Nehru by setting up a trust known as Nehru Memorial Education Society under the distinguished president ship of late C.K.Nair.
